# Product Overview

## One-line pitch

FPL Dashboard is an all-in-one decision cockpit for Fantasy Premier League managers, combining live score tracking, rank context, fixture planning, captaincy support, and transfer review in a single interface.

## Who it is for

### Primary audience
- serious mini-league players
- rank-focused FPL managers
- active weekly planners who want more context than the official app provides

### Secondary audience
- engaged casual managers trying to improve consistency
- builders, analysts, and FPL content creators who want a cleaner data view

## Problems it solves

### 1. Matchday tracking is fragmented
Users often need to switch between multiple FPL screens to understand:
- current score
- whether points are live or final
- rank implications
- which players have played or are still pending

### 2. Weekly planning lacks context
Captaincy, fixture runs, blanks, doubles, and transfer reflection are hard to evaluate in one place.

### 3. Decision review is weak
Managers rarely get a clean read on whether transfer decisions were actually beneficial once hits, chips, and future fixtures are considered.

## Core product value

### Live clarity
- live / provisional / official gameweek lifecycle
- rank and score awareness in one dashboard
- auto-sub style projected logic for squad understanding

### Planning intelligence
- 5-GW fixture ticker
- DGW/BGW support
- captaincy adviser
- market target surfacing

### Decision review
- transfer analyser with hit-adjusted value context
- gameweek history and season trajectory

### League context
- league standings
- live league view

## Differentiation

Compared with the official FPL interface, this product aims to provide:

- more decision context in fewer clicks
- better visibility into live status and rank movement
- stronger fixture-driven planning support
- clearer post-decision analysis of transfers and chips
