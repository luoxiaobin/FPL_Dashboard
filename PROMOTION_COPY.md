## Short product summary

FPL Dashboard helps Fantasy Premier League managers track live score status, understand rank context, review transfer impact, and plan upcoming gameweeks with fixture intelligence and captaincy support.

## Launch post: short

Built a new FPL dashboard that brings live score tracking, rank projection, captaincy advice, fixture ticker, DGW/BGW awareness, league context, and transfer analysis into one interface.

It is designed for managers who want faster weekly decisions and better matchday visibility than the official UI alone.

## Launch post: medium

I have been building an FPL dashboard focused on one thing: making weekly decisions clearer.

It combines:

- live / provisional / official GW score tracking
- rank projection and season context
- DGW/BGW-aware fixture ticker
- captaincy adviser and market target view
- transfer analyser with hit-adjusted value context
- league standings and live league view

The goal is to make live tracking, planning, and review feel like one workflow rather than five separate screens.
